Beyond the Moon

Beyond distance, beyond waiting, there is a place I made for you. Not a page. A place. A realm where our love has already taken root.

the threshold

Some loves arrive like moonlight.

Quietly. Softly. Then all at once, they are everywhere. Somewhere between Germany and Brazil, I imagine this place — un lugar where distance cannot enter, and where every part of me keeps reaching for you. Te pienso, mi amor. Even here, especially here.

the admiration grove

What I admire in you.

Not just how beautiful you are — though God knows you are. I admire the things deeper than that. The things that stay.

Your softness

I admire how your heart still knows how to be gentle. The world can be harsh, but you still carry tenderness in you, and that is rare.

Your strength

I admire the way you keep going even when things feel heavy. There is a quiet strength in you that does not need to shout to be real.

Your mind

I admire the way you think, the way you feel deeply, the way your presence can make a simple conversation become the best part of my day.

Your light

I admire how your smile changes the room in my mind. Even from far away, you still manage to feel like sunlight finding me.

Your heart

I admire how much love there is in you. How much care. How much sweetness. How much life. It makes me want to protect that heart with both hands.

The artist in you

I admire that your hands know how to create. That you can look at the world and turn feeling into form. There is something sacred about a person who notices beauty deeply enough to draw it.

You, entirely

Most of all, I admire that you are you. Not some perfect fantasy — but you, with your real self, your real soul, and all the beautiful things that make me keep choosing you.

the ancient willow

It grows because of you.

The willow keeps what I could never bear to lose: the little moments, the waiting, the hope, and the way loving you made something quiet in me come alive.

Like a sketch touched by patience.

and is unforgettable anyway.

If I could draw what loving you feels like, I think the page would still fall short. Some things are too alive to stay inside lines.

Not because time is short, but because every little dream with you feels worth naming.

✦

Have quiet mornings, sleepy laughs, long hugs, and days where distance is finally just an old enemy we defeated.

beneath the branches

What the willow was trying to say.

There are things I could say quickly, but they deserve a quieter place.

✧

Mi amor,

Three months may sound small to the world, but to me it already holds so much. It holds the conversations, the waiting, the smiles, the little moments that became important before I even realized they were changing me.

Loving you from far away has taught me something real: love does not need to be loud to be powerful. Sometimes it grows quietly, like roots beneath water — hidden, patient, alive.

I think about the day I finally get to hold you, and it feels unreal in the best way. Like all this distance, all this missing you, all this patience will finally become something I can touch.

You are not just someone I love. You are someone I am growing toward. And if this little realm means anything, let it mean this: even from far away, I am choosing you with my whole heart. Te elijo. Ahora y siempre.

Happy three months, my princess.
I love you.
— Jadon

the vow chamber

The ultimate vow.

Not perfection. Not fantasy. Just something honest, steady, and chosen.

I do not promise a perfect story.

I promise honesty, even when the truth is difficult.
I promise patience while distance is still trying to test us.
I promise effort, because what we have deserves more than convenience.
I promise to keep learning how to love you better, not just louder.
I promise that my heart will keep returning to you, on the easy days and the hard ones too.
Te elijo — not only in the beautiful moments, but in the waiting, the learning, and the becoming.
Beneath this willow, before the moon, I choose you with intention. Ahora y siempre.

sealed beneath the willow

Pinky promise?

Across the distance, through every waiting day — let this be our little promise.

I choose you — softly, fully, and on purpose.
