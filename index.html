the letter

What I really wanted to say.

This part is simple on purpose. No big performance. Just me.

Mi amor,

Three months might not sound like a lot to everybody else, but it means a lot to me. It means we've already made something real enough for me to care this deeply.

I know I joke around a lot, and I know I can hide how serious I actually am sometimes, but with you I don't want to pretend. I care about you for real.

I love your softness, your mind, the way you care, the way you create, the way your whole presence can make my day feel lighter. I think about you more than I probably know how to explain properly.

I don't just want to give you something pretty. I want to give you something that feels intentional. Something that says I didn't just think of you — I sat down and made time for you.

So that's what this is. A little place under the willow. A reminder that even with distance, I am still here. Still choosing you. Still growing toward you.

Happy three months, mi amor.
— Jadon
